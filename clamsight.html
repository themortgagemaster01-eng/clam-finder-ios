How the AI helps
When you scan, Clam Finder reads the live video for the tell-tale signs experienced clammers look for:
Siphon holes — small dark dimples or "keyholes" where a clam's neck reaches the surface.
Sand disturbances — little donuts, bumps, or dimples in otherwise smooth sand.
Squirts & movement — a clam breathing can spit water as you walk past.
You can also teach it your sand (in Scan) so it learns what real signs look like where you dig. It's a helper, not a guarantee — always dig gently to confirm.

About & safety

Harvest safely & legally

Shellfish filter their surroundings, so they concentrate bacteria and biotoxins from polluted or uncertified water. Harvesting from closed areas can cause serious illness.

Always: harvest only from certified/open waters, check current closures (rain often triggers temporary closures), keep only legal-size shellfish, observe daily limits, and carry any required permit.
